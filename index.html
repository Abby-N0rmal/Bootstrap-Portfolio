About Me

In the past I have tinkered with code when I ran a video game server and had to add in mods for our gaming community. I edited and helped create scripts, and we used github to share the code. This got me extremely interested in coding outside of games.

After working in the construction industry and seeing the need for applications that could be implemented for inventory systems that would greatly improve these companies, I decided to persue further education in development.

I'm very artistic, and I love to put my imagination and ideas to good use and particularly enjoy the front end aspects of coding, the things you can see that engage the user!
